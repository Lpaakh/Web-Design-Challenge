Summary: Latitude vs. Weather

This project examined the latitude versus four weather elements: cloudiness, humidity, temperature and wind speed. Data was pulled from the OpenWeatherMap API to collect information from varying city latitudes spanning the globe.

After gathering the data, Matplotlib was used to visualize the latitude versus the four prior mentioned weather elements. This website takes a look at the analysis, four weather elements versus latitude graphs with correlations and the source dataset that was used to form the analysis.
